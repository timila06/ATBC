Mission & Vision

Clear purpose for Australia-Thailand business growth.

ATBC exists to promote practical trade, investment, and partnership opportunities between Australia and Thailand through trusted networks and focused council activity.

Vision

A stronger Australia-Thailand relationship.

To be a leading non-profit organisation strengthening trade, investment, and collaboration between Thailand and Australia, while supporting members with meaningful bilateral opportunities.

Mission

Create practical business opportunities.

To connect members with relevant business networks, government and industry stakeholders, policy insight, and market access pathways that support sustainable bilateral growth.

How ATBC delivers
