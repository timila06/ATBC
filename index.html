About us

Built for trade, investment, and trusted introductions.

The ATBC strengthens connections between the Australian and Thai private sectors, fostering mutually beneficial partnerships and promoting opportunities between both countries.

Established in 1980

With representatives connected across Sydney, Perth, Adelaide, Brisbane, and Bangkok, the Council provides access to government and industry leaders, market intelligence, and high-value networking.

A stronger, more connected Australia-Thailand relationship.
