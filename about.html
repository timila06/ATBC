About ATBC

The leading bilateral business organisation for Australia and Thailand.

The ATBC strengthens connections between Australian and Thai private sectors, fostering mutually beneficial partnerships and promoting business opportunities between the two countries.

History

Established in 1980.

The Australia Thailand Business Council promotes commercial cooperation, facilitates business connections, and advocates on behalf of its members. With representatives based in Sydney, Perth, Adelaide, Brisbane, and Bangkok, the Council provides access to influential government and industry leaders, market intelligence, and high-value networking opportunities.

Vision

A stronger Australia-Thailand relationship.

ATBC aims to be a leading non-profit organisation strengthening trade, investment, education, and collaboration between Thailand and Australia.

Mission

Practical opportunities across business, trade, and education.

Through trade missions, exhibitions, roadshows, networking events, student programs, internships, scholarships, and study opportunities, ATBC fosters stronger people-to-people connections and long-term cooperation.

Strategic partners

Networks across Australia, Thailand, and ASEAN.
