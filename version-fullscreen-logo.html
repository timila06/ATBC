About us

Built for trade, investment, and trusted introductions.

The ATBC strengthens connections between the Australian and Thai private sectors, fostering mutually beneficial partnerships and promoting opportunities between both countries.

Established in 1980

With representatives connected across Sydney, Perth, Adelaide, Brisbane, and Bangkok, the Council provides access to government and industry leaders, market intelligence, and high-value networking.

Council role

Access, insight, and advocacy for bilateral business.

ATBC provides a platform for business leaders and representatives from both nations to connect, exchange ideas, and collaborate on initiatives that support bilateral trade and investment.

Mission and vision

Clear priorities for a stronger Australia-Thailand relationship.

Vision: to be a leading non-profit organisation strengthening trade, investment, and collaboration between Thailand and Australia.

Mission: to create opportunities through business networking, trade missions, roadshows, education exchange, internships, scholarships, and long-term people-to-people cooperation.

Business Networking and Engagement

Organise networking events that connect business leaders and stakeholders from Thailand and Australia while building relationships with potential partner organisations.
